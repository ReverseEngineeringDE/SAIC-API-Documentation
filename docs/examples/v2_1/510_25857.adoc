=== A/C
(See com.saic.cmp.business.home_page.air_conditioner.AirConditionerModel):

==== A/C On

* rcvReqType: 0x06
* rcvParams:
** paramId: 19
** paramValue: 0x02
*** Start A/C seems to be 0x02
*** Start Fron/defrost seems to be 0x05
** paramId: 20
** paramValue: 0x08
*** Temperature byte, depends on car model (See com.saic.cmp.business.home_page.air_conditioner.View.AirConditionerAdapter) 0x08 is 22Â° in MG5
** paramId: 255
** paramValue: 0x00

==== A/C Off
* rcvReqType: 0x06
* rcvParams:
** paramId: 19
** paramValue: 0x00
** paramId: 20
** paramValue: 0x00
** paramId: 255
** paramValue: 0x00
